Republic Day Celebration

The 67th Republic Day was celebrated at JNV, Mana-Camp, Raipur on 26/01/2016 with great joy and pomp. Principal, Vice-Principal, Teachers with students assembled in the parade ground where Principal hoisted the Indian Flag with potential and energy. After that the parade ground also witnessed for the encouraging march past in which students took part on the rhythm of drum bits. Apart from that sudents also participated in number of other activities i.e. Lazium, Dumb'bell, Free Hand Experience.

Our Social Science teacher Mr. O.P. Chourasia (PGT History) addressed the mass with heart touching story of soldiers and made the people remember who sacrificed their life in the freedom movement. Vice Principal (Smt. Jyoti Singh) emphasised on the sacrifice of two students of JNV, Mana-Camp, Raipur Late Shri Vivek Shukla (Sub Inspector) and Late Shri Mahendra Singh Dhruw (DSP) who had sacrificed their life serving for the nation. The programme was coordinatede by Shri Shubhash Bharti (TGT Hindi) and Shri P.G. Behere (Music Teacher). Vote of thanks was presented by Mr. G.B. Guru (PGT English) in which he added that every body should think for the nation first then about family and lastly about himself.
